Hey Ocean Superheroes welcome !

We are a team of passionate individuals who are committed to protecting the oceans from pollution and its impact on marine life. We believe that everyone has a role to play in saving our oceans, and we are dedicated to providing the information and tools that people need to make a difference.

Features
  • Get real-time information about water quality and the presence of nearby endangered creatures.
  • Learn how to protect endangered creatures and reduce your impact on the environment.
  • Compete with friends and family on a leaderboard to see who can complete the most environmentally friendly activities.
  • Use machine learning to predict water quality and the next endangered species.
